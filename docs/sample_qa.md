### 2. Factual Query: Investment Objective

**User:** Investment objective of HDFC Large Cap Fund?
**Assistant:** The investment objective of HDFC Large Cap Fund is to provide long-term capital appreciation/income by investing predominantly in large-cap companies. There is no assurance that the investment objective of the scheme will be achieved.

Last updated from sources: <https://files.hdfcfund.com/s3fs-public/Others/2026-02/Fund%20Facts%20-%20HDFC%20Large%20Cap%20Fund_January%2026.pdf>

### 3. Factual Query: Exit Load

**User:** What is the exit load for HDFC Large Cap Fund?
**Assistant:** The exit load for HDFC Large Cap Fund is 1.00% if units are redeemed or switched out within one year from the date of allotment. No exit load is payable if units are redeemed or switched out after one year from the date of allotment.

Last updated from sources: <https://files.hdfcfund.com/s3fs-public/SID/2025-11/SID%20-%20HDFC%20Large%20Cap%20Fund%20dated%20November%2021%2C%202025_0.pdf>

### 4. Factual Query: ELSS Lock-in

**User:** ELSS lock-in period?
**Assistant:** HDFC ELSS Tax Saver has a mandatory lock-in period of 3 years from the date of allotment of units. This is as per the Equity Linked Savings Scheme (ELSS) guidelines issued by the Government of India.

Last updated from sources: <https://files.hdfcfund.com/s3fs-public/KIM/2025-11/KIM%20-%20HDFC%20ELSS%20Tax%20Saver%20dated%20November%2021%2C%202025_0.pdf>

**User:** Minimum SIP for HDFC Balanced Advantage Fund?
