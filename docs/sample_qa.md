# HDFC Mutual Fund Assistant - Sample Q&A

This document contains sample interactions with the RAG system to demonstrate its behavior and compliance with constraints.

### 1. Factual Query: Exit Load

**User:** What is the exit load for HDFC Top 100?
**Assistant:** The exit load is 1% if units are redeemed or switched out within one year from the date of allotment. No exit load is applicable for redemptions after one year.
Last updated from sources: February 2026
Source: <https://www.hdfcfund.com/explore/mutual-funds/hdfc-large-cap-fund/direct>

### 2. Factual Query: ELSS Lock-in

**User:** ELSS lock-in period?
**Assistant:** HDFC ELSS Tax Saver has a mandatory lock-in period of 3 years from the date of allotment of units. This is as per the Equity Linked Savings Scheme (ELSS) guidelines issued by the Government of India.
Last updated from sources: February 2026
Source: <https://files.hdfcfund.com/s3fs-public/KIM/2025-11/KIM%20-%20HDFC%20ELSS%20Tax%20Saver%20dated%20November%2021%2C%202025_0.pdf>
